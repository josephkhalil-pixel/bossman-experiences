FAQ

Questions, Answered

Quick answers for entry, location, vendors and event details.

Is entry free?

Yes, entry to Mounties Winterfest is free. Food, drinks and vendor items are purchased directly from vendors.

Do I need a ticket?

No ticket is required for general entry unless stated otherwise for a specific promotion or future event.

Where is the event?

Mounties Carpark, 254 Humphries Rd, Mount Pritchard NSW.

Can I apply as a vendor?

Yes. Visit the Apply as a Vendor page and submit your details. The Bossman team will review your request and contact suitable applicants.

Are event details subject to change?

Yes. Vendor lineups, event features, weather arrangements and timings may change depending on operational requirements, venue conditions and safety needs.
