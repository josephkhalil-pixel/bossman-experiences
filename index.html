Who we are

Bossman Experiences brings together Western Sydney’s best food vendors, desserts, drinks, entertainment and community energy in one place. Our events are designed to feel bold, local and easy to attend.

Our mission

To create food events that celebrate local culture, support vendors and give people a reason to come together.

What we do

We curate food festivals, vendor lineups, giveaways, event announcements and on-ground experiences for the community.

FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything guests usually ask before pulling up to a Bossman event.

Is entry free?

Yes. Mounties Winterfest is free entry, no ticket required.

Where is the event?

Mounties Carpark, 254 Humphries Rd, Mount Pritchard NSW 2170.

Is there parking?

Yes, free on-site parking is available at Mounties. Arrive early on busy nights.

Are vendors cash or card?

Most vendors accept card, but availability may vary. Bringing both card and cash is recommended.

Is it family friendly?

Yes. Bossman events are designed for families, friends and food lovers.

How do I become a vendor?

Use the Apply as Vendor form and the team will review your request.
